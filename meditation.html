MEDITATION FOR BEGINNERS
"STOP, RELAX and BREATHE a way to realse any stress and anxiety"

when meditating it is very important to feel relaxed and able to feel comfortable in the space you are in. As we all know lockdown has been very tough but even with the lock down meditation is a key and important to do in our everyday life.

Focus on your breathing

Try to keep your attention and mind on breathing in and out. Inhale and exhale.

Feel your Breathe

Notice how your breath goes in and out
