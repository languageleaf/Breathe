Meditation for Beginners

"STOP, RELAX and BREATHE a way to realse any stress and anxiety"

When meditating it is very important to feel relaxed and able to feel comfortable in the space you are in. As we all know lockdown has been very tough but even with the lock down meditation is a key and important to do in our everyday life.

  1. Focus on your breathing.

    2. Try to keep your attention and mind on breathing in and out. Inhale and exhale.

  2. Feel your breath.

    3. Notice how your breath goes in and out

  3. Being comfortable and relaxed.

    4. Simply focus on being you, being comfortable in your environment and feeling relaxed and care-free.

There are many different types of ways as to how people meditates here are 5 different types people mediates:

  1. Listening to music
  2. Colouring in or drawing
  3. Exercise
  4. Yoga
  5. Reading

We practice mindfulness so we are able to recognise how our minds are moving everyday. Taking deep breaths and finding a relaxing atmosphere is always an important way to ease everything on your mind.
